How to Play Resemble (Cognify)

  1. Analyze the Source Figure: A pattern of colored shapes appears on the left.
  2. Check Instructions: Look for instructions like "rotate 90°" or "reflect," which tell you how to alter the figure.
  3. Visualize the Transformation: Mentally rotate or flip the original shape as instructed.
  4. Recreate on the Grid: Use the available building blocks. Double-click the mixed block in the palette (or in the grid) to rotate it.
  5. Submit Quickly: Complete as many patterns as possible within the time limit (6 Minutes).
